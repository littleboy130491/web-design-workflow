# Concept A

## Concept Name

Measured Steel

## One-Sentence Visual Thesis

A disciplined industrial direction that uses rigid structure, controlled contrast, and proof-first imagery to make the company feel organized, credible, and execution-ready.

## Style Keywords

- disciplined
- structural
- credible
- measured
- site-ready

## Typography Direction

- Primary display: `IBM Plex Sans Condensed`
- Supporting text: `IBM Plex Sans`
- Tone: compact, technical, and highly legible
- Heading behavior: tall uppercase or title-case headlines with strong weight contrast

## Color Direction

- Background: `#E7E1D6` limestone
- Primary dark: `#22262B` graphite
- Secondary neutral: `#7D7F76` concrete gray
- Accent: `#E5742A` safety orange
- Text light: `#F7F4EE` chalk white

## Spacing And Density Direction

- Medium-tight spacing
- Narrow vertical rhythm with deliberate margin changes
- Dense enough to feel operational, not cramped

## Imagery Direction

- Cropped construction details, structural frames, site teams, concrete, steel, and facade work
- Images should feel practical and evidence-led rather than cinematic
- Imagery acts as proof blocks, not decoration

## Composition Direction

- One dominant upright grid with a strong leading image panel
- Supporting zones should feel like labeled technical specimens
- The board should read like a disciplined presentation sheet, not a website screen

## Surface Treatment Direction

- Hard edges or minimal radius
- Thin rules, restrained panels, low-shadow surfaces
- Texture should suggest concrete dust, blueprint paper, or coated stock without becoming noisy

## AI Stylescape Prompt Spec

- Use case: `ui-mockup`
- Asset type: visual-direction board for a construction company website
- Primary request: create a polished stylescape board for a dependable Jakarta construction firm, showing disciplined typography specimens, proof-led imagery crops, structured color swatches, and industrial surface treatments without becoming a homepage mockup
- Scene or backdrop: editorial presentation board on a limestone paper-like background with graphite framing
- Subject: disciplined industrial web-brand direction for commercial and industrial construction
- Style or medium: premium raster stylescape, editorial brand board, realistic materials, no device frame
- Composition or framing: portrait board, one dominant image column, specimen type blocks, color chips, material strip, layout fragments, strong vertical grid
- Lighting or mood: even studio presentation light, calm, serious, controlled
- Color palette: limestone, graphite, concrete gray, safety orange, chalk white
- Constraints: stylescape board only, no full webpage, no long readable paragraphs, no fake contact details, no repeated generic cards, no decorative icon clutter
- Avoid: template website screenshot, laptop mockup, excessive neon, glossy corporate stock look, malformed text, noisy infographic clutter

## Prompt Iteration Note

If the result starts to look like a full homepage or dashboard, tighten the prompt toward `editorial stylescape board only`, reduce UI framing, and replace readable copy with short specimen labels or abstract typographic blocks.

## Generic-Risk Note

This direction can become generic if the orange accent is overused or if the grid turns into a bland software dashboard instead of a construction-focused board.

## Best Fit Note

Best for procurement teams, developers, and operators who want immediate signals of control, clarity, and execution discipline.

## Anti-Pattern Note

Do not turn this concept into hazard-stripe theatrics, heavy black-and-orange branding, or a dense ERP-style interface.
