# Concept B

## Concept Name

Civic Craft

## One-Sentence Visual Thesis

A warmer, more editorial direction that makes the company feel mature, trustworthy, and reassuring without losing construction credibility.

## Style Keywords

- grounded
- mature
- editorial
- tactile
- reassuring

## Typography Direction

- Primary display: `Newsreader`
- Supporting text: `Manrope`
- Tone: calm, assured, and premium without luxury signaling
- Heading behavior: generous serif headlines paired with compact, practical sans-serif details

## Color Direction

- Background: `#F3EEE4` chalk stone
- Primary dark: `#2E322B` moss charcoal
- Secondary warm: `#A56A4A` fired clay
- Accent: `#C9B38D` sand brass
- Text light: `#FCFAF6` warm ivory

## Spacing And Density Direction

- Spacious spacing with clear breathing room
- Fewer, larger zones instead of many smaller modules
- Calm rhythm that still preserves scan speed

## Imagery Direction

- Daylight project photography, tactile material crops, completed spaces, and restrained human presence
- Images should feel trustworthy and real rather than gritty or dramatic
- Imagery supports atmosphere and confidence more than urgency

## Composition Direction

- One dominant editorial asymmetry with wide margins and a strong hero crop
- Type, imagery, and swatches should feel curated like a presentation board
- The board should emphasize calm hierarchy over operational density

## Surface Treatment Direction

- Soft paper texture, thin borders, quiet frames, minimal shadow
- Slightly softened corners are acceptable, but no pill-shaped UI
- Surface language should feel tactile and composed rather than slick

## AI Stylescape Prompt Spec

- Use case: `ui-mockup`
- Asset type: visual-direction board for a construction company website
- Primary request: create a polished stylescape board for a reliable Indonesian construction firm, showing editorial typography specimens, warm daylight project imagery, tactile color swatches, and calm layout fragments without becoming a finished homepage
- Scene or backdrop: warm paper-like presentation board with soft mineral tones and curated image crops
- Subject: mature trust-first web-brand direction for commercial and industrial construction
- Style or medium: premium raster stylescape, editorial moodboard, tactile materials, no device frame
- Composition or framing: portrait board, asymmetrical layout, large serif headline specimen, one main image anchor, smaller detail crops, color chips, spacing specimens
- Lighting or mood: natural daylight, grounded, confident, reassuring
- Color palette: chalk stone, moss charcoal, fired clay, sand brass, warm ivory
- Constraints: stylescape board only, not a full webpage, keep copy minimal and neutral, no boutique hotel styling, no luxury fashion cues, no fake project details
- Avoid: generic real-estate brochure, overly soft lifestyle collage, excessive beige minimalism, readable lorem ipsum blocks, template hero screenshot

## Prompt Iteration Note

If the result feels too residential, too hospitality-like, or too soft, push the prompt toward `commercial construction proof`, tighten the sans-serif utility details, and increase the presence of material and structural imagery.

## Generic-Risk Note

This direction can become generic if it drifts into beige editorial minimalism without enough operational proof and construction context.

## Best Fit Note

Best for business owners and decision-makers who need the company to feel safe, mature, and easy to trust before they inspect technical details.

## Anti-Pattern Note

Do not let this concept become boutique real estate marketing, hospitality branding, or decorative lifestyle minimalism.
